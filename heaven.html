FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is food included in the hostel fees?
Yes! All three meals - Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner - are included in the hostel fees. We prepare home-cooked, nutritious meals daily using fresh ingredients.
Are the rooms fully furnished?
Yes, all our rooms are fully furnished with comfortable beds, pillows, blankets, study tables, chairs, and storage facilities. We maintain high hygiene standards with regular cleaning.
Is WiFi available in the rooms?
Absolutely! We provide high-speed WiFi connectivity in all rooms and common areas. The internet connection is fast and reliable throughout the day and night.
Is the hostel safe for working women?
Yes, safety is our top priority. We have 24/7 security personnel, CCTV surveillance in all common areas, strict visitor policy, and trained staff dedicated to ensuring the safety and comfort of all residents.
What are the different room sharing options?
We offer six room types: Single Sharing (private), Double Sharing (2 beds), Triple Sharing (3 beds), 5 Sharing (5 beds), 6 Sharing (6 beds), and Dormitory. Choose the option that suits your budget and preferences.
How do I book a room?
Simply call us at +91 98940 22885 or WhatsApp to enquire about availability. You can also visit our hostel in Peelamedu to view the rooms and finalize your booking. We make the process quick and hassle-free.
